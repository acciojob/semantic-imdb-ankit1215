Summary

The Shawshank Redemption is a story of hope and friendship set in a prison. Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongly convicted of murder, spends decades forming a bond with fellow inmate Red. Despite the brutal conditions of Shawshank Prison, Andy maintains his dignity and never loses hope, eventually orchestrating a daring escape that changes both his and Red’s life forever.
